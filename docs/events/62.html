Quixort




Campers, welcome to Quixort. It's the best Jackbox game. It's fun. It's collaborative. It's skill. It's luck. For those of you who don't know, let me explain: Quixort is a block based trivia game that involves stacking blocks in their correct order based on the left to right scale given to you. An example might be "Most populated countries" where the game will give you blocks one by one and you will need to correctly stack and place the blocks in the correct order to keep going. For this event, your cabin will have the entire week to schedule two sessions of "Play Forever" mode with Ali to determine the Quixort champion. The rules are quite simple:

- Two attempts of "Play Forever" (can be non-consecutive, I will give you a few practice rounds if requested) anytime during the week Ali is available
- Cabins will be ranked on their highest score between the two attemptsÂ 
- Any cabin member can participate and help
- You do NOT need to have every participating cabin member help control the blocks. It is optional.
- No attempting to use outside sources to help


1st place will be awarded 200 points
2nd place will be awarded 150 points
3rd place will be awarded 100 points
4th place will be awarded 50 points
