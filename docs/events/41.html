Golf with Your Friends



Welcome to the whatever Annual Smash Camp Golf with Your Friends Invitational. The basic requirement to joining this event is owning the game "Golf with Your Friends" on Steam. Do not worry too much about your timezone, as there will be multiple lobbies throughout the week so we can get everyone interested to get a shot. We will be playing on the same 18 hole course (to be revealed later) and the top five scoring participants will earn the following:

1st place will be awarded 100 points
2nd place will be awarded 75 points
3rd place will be awarded 40 points
4th place will be awarded 30 points
5th place will be awarded 20 points
NOTE: If turn out is better than expected, I will award 6-10th place 10 points each.

Do note that only the first time you participate in the lobby will be the score that we accept for placements. Additionally, we will only be taking scores from a certain amount of people per cabin going by whatever cabin has the least amount of participants. This means that if one cabin has 5 participants and another has 3, only the 3 highest scoring per cabin will count. Do not let that discourage you from participating - if you have more active people than other cabins, it still means you have more chances to get a better score!

Rules

Max shots per round: Infinite
Max time per round: 2 minutes (120 seconds)
Max time penalty: 3
Spawn position: Default
Collision Mode: Disabled
Powerups mode: Disabled
Flag Indicator: Default
Free-cam time: 15
Retry shot mode: Disabled
Ball spin: Disabled
Allow Jumping: Enabled
Everything else is default settings
