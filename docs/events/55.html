Video Game Challenge Event



Welcome everyone back to the video game challenge run! Honestly this was a massive success last year and I thought about maybe imposing a theme or something for this year, but the rest of the leaders said it was perfect the way it was and they’re right! So it’s a complete runback. Only thing I’d note is we still have access to all the challenge runs from 2023, so if you’re a returning camper who submitted a run, your run better not be similar to your last one. As a refresher, this challenge asks you to perform a feat or challenge in a video game, and submit it. This challenge or feat must be premeditated, and it obviously cannot be an old video. "Challenge or feat" is purposefully vague - I'm looking for you to do something impressive or otherwise notable in a way you're putting your own personal flair on it. If you're lost for ideas, ask your cabinmates for ideas and give them your favorite game and see if they can come up with something.

Here are some examples of what you COULD do:
  • Beat a boss while handicapping yourself in some way
  • Speedrun a level or chapter of a campaign with an additional challenge
  • Trickshot or gimmick play in a multiplayer game
  • Reach an achievement in a funny or unexpected way

What I don't want to see:
  • A straight up boss kill (Do something funny or epic or something to spice it up)
  • A random pop off play (I want to see a planned idea executed)
  • Any submission without proof its you (Did your brother do this?)

Record this run in whatever way you're able and upload it to us. Make sure that we know it's you in your run by noting your identity somehow - show your username on console, show steam if on pc, etc. If you're using a console such as PS or Xbox, I recommend you use the inbuilt streaming to twitch to record your footage. If you're playing a game on the PC or in your browser etc, record your screen. If you're playing a game on your switch or for some other reason can't do anything else, record that shit on a device with a camera and upload it. We won't judge based on presentation quality, just the quality of your run! You have the whole week to work on this submission and it will be due at the end of Day 5. 

1st place will be awarded 150 points
2nd place will be awarded 100 points
3rd place will be awarded 75 points
4th and 5th place will be awarded 50 points
6th through 10th place will be awarded 20 points
