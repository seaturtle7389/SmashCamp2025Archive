Video Game Geoguessr (Day 1)

We will be providing screenshots from these 3 games: Super Mario Odyssey, Monster Hunter Wilds, and Pokemon Scarlet/Violet. You will try to figure out where the character is standing on the map based off the screenshot. You will submit your answer as a cabin from these map websites we're providing:

SCREENSHOTS!
Mario: Kingdom Maps - Super Mario Odyssey Guide - IGN
Monster Hunter: Interactive Map and List of Locations | Monster Hunter Wilds｜Game8
Pokemon: Paldea Region | Pokémon Scarlet/Violet Map | Map Genie

Points will be awarded for each location based off closest to farthest. If there is a situation in which there are answers from different cabins that are so close to each other the difference is negligible, it will be a tie and both cabins (or more) will receive the same amount of points

1st - 30 points
2nd - 20 points
3rd - 10 points
4th - 5 points

But that's not all! Along with that, there are also 10 screenshots from different games where you have to guess the video game and what level/location in the game the image is from. Cabins will get 5 points for guessing the correct game and 5 points for guessing the correct level/location.

SCREENSHOTS!

Answers will be submitted in the submissions channel for each cabin. 

DO NOT CHEAT BY USING GOOGLE LENS OR AND REVERSE IMAGE SEARCHES! THAT IS LAME!
