Mario Kart 8 Deluxe Tournament



The Mario Kart 8 Deluxe tournament is returning for a third year. We considered Mario Kart World, but have decided to keep the tournament on 8 Deluxe - keep a look out for some Mario Kart World lobbies and maybe some other World somewhere else. As per the last couple years, we're running the event on either Day 3 or Day 4, at the time listed below.

The time this year will be 6:00 PM, (in your timezone, the website auto-converts it for you c:) and I want contestants to vote on doing it on day 3, 4, or 5. If you are interested in participating, please sign up in this thread and indicate which days you can participate in the three games.

Indicate which days you can do on signup for the event. Three per cabin will race for a full lobby of 12 racers for 12 races - This should be great again.

Rules

In game settings:
  • 12 players
  • 3 reps per cabin
  • 12 races played, scored as a team
  • Tracks: All Random

Scoring


Every cabin's TOTAL points will be tallied at the end of the 12 races, and scoring is based on the team with the most points.

1st place will be awarded 250 points
2nd place will be awarded 150 points
3rd place will be awarded 100 points
4th place will be awarded 50 points
