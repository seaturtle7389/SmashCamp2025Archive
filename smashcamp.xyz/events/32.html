The Smash Camp Relay



Welcome Smash Campers to the Video Game Relay. This event is the spiritual successor to Speedrun events and the Pokemon bingo. We’ve picked 6 mini-speedruns for your cabin to complete by the end of the week. Scoring will be based on your cabin’s total time tallied across all games, in addition to a point bonus to every camper who has the fastest run of the game they did. Since this is a RELAY, we are mandating that a different camper takes each game. To be specific, you are allowed to participate in ONE run, or you may participate in TWO runs if at least one of the runs is a Co-Op run. Pretty simple I think, but don’t hesitate to ask if anything is unclear! Without further ado, here’s the games you’re running to help you plan. The goal you are completing in each game will be revealed on Day 1.

Super Mario 64 (Nintendo 64, Nintendo Switch Online, Emulator, Any Virtual Console, NOT the DS version)
The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom (Nintendo Switch, Nintendo Switch 2*)
Splatoon 3 (Nintendo Switch, Nintendo Switch 2**)
Elden Ring (Any system)
Left 4 Dead 2, Two player Co-Op (Steam)
R.E.P.O, One to Four player Co-Op (Steam)

*The Switch 2 version of Tears of the Kingdom is allowed with a 60 second time penalty to compensate for differences
**Playing Splatoon 3 on a Switch 2 is allowed with a 30 second time penalty to compensate for differences

SPEEDRUN DETAILS FOR EACH GAME

Super Mario 64
Goal - Collect any 16 Stars on a fresh save file.
Time start - Upon starting a new file
Time stop - Cutscene for collecting 16th star

Tears of the Kingdom
Goal - Complete two shrines on Great Sky Island.
Time start - Upon gaining the ability to move at the start of the game (SAVE right after the opening cutscene on new file, and load it to start run)
Time stop - Completion cutscene of your 2nd shrine

Splatoon 3
Goal - Defeat DJ Octavio in Alterna on a fresh save.
Time start - Upon gaining movement post Cap'n Cuttlefish conversation that occurs when entering Alterna for the first time
Time stop - Cutscene for defeating DJ Octavio

Elden Ring
Goal - Defeat the bosses Flying Dragon Agheel, Leonine Misbegotten (Castle Morne) and Erdtree Avatar (Weeping Peninsula Minor Erdtree) in any order, with NO online summons.
Time start - Upon creating new save
Time stop - Your last boss' health bar reaches 0

Left 4 Dead 2 Co-Op
Goal - Clear the Dead Center campaign (Levels 1-4) on Normal difficulty in a group of 2.
Time start - Upon gaining movement on top of the hotel in level 1
Time stop - Cutscene when you finish level 4

R.E.P.O. Co-Op
Goal - Clear Level 3 in a group of 2-4.
Time start - Upon all players gaining movement at the start of Level 1
Time stop - When screen goes black after the van leaves Level 3

Rules

  • You are not allowed to use cheats, mods, etc during your run.
  • Each cabin member is only allowed to submit ONE solo run. You may also participate in an additional Co-Op run, or participate in both the Co-Op runs without a solo run.
  • If using an emulator to play, your emulator must be locked at 100% speed for the duration of the run.
  • To submit your run, record your run using game capture on PC, your phone, whatever works as long as we can clearly see the run. DM your submission and screenshots to @wheegalure.

You have all week to complete runs for the Smash Camp Relay. Total times will be tallied and points awarded upon Day 5 conclusion.

Scoring

1st place will be awarded 300 points
2nd place will be awarded 200 points
3rd place will be awarded 100 points
4th place will be awarded 50 points
1st placers in every individual game will be awarded 25 bonus points
