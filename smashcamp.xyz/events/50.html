Name 100 Women Challenge

We have so many events we wanted to start you guys off a little bit light today for a group event.

You will be using this nifty website: Can You Name 100 Women?

It is up to your discretion how many people you want to be available for this event. There will be a designated person entering the names on the website while streaming in the cabin voice chat. The other people in the call can help the designated typer with filling out the list by naming women. Once you successfully name 100 women as a cabin, you are finished. You MUST coordinate a time to start the event with an admin and let them know who is participating. Other cabin members that are not participating can join the call, but they must be muted when the event begins. 

Placements will of course be based on which cabin completed the challenge the fastest. 

tl;dr rules: 
-There will be 1 designated typist. 
-Up to the cabin how many people they want available in call. 
-Names must be given verbally in call by those helping. People cannot type it out and the typist cannot just copy and paste the name. 
-No adult stars. If adult stars are entered, there will be a time penalty of 10 seconds for each one. 
-You must coordinate a start time with a leader to be present for when you begin your run. 

1st place will be awarded 150 points
2nd place will be awarded 100 points
3rd place will be awarded 75 points
4th place will be awarded 50 points
