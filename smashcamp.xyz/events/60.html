Super Auto Pets Tournament



By popular demand (me), we're bringing it. I'm talking about Super Auto Pets babyyyyyyy. This year we are looking to bring in a new tournament that almost anybody could play and has just the right amount of skill/luck to make the entire camp invested. So get ready, we're running this Mario Kart style - we're going to grab 2 campers from each cabin and run an 8 person lobby and score based on how the cabin does as a whole, as opposed to a normal bracket tournament. This game is better played in a group than individually, so there may be some scheduling conflicts.

The time this year will be [TBD], (in your timezone, the website auto-converts it for you c:) and I want contestants to vote on doing it on day 2, 3, or 4. If you are interested in participating, please sign up in this thread and indicate which days you can participate in the race.

Rules

  • 3 Matches
  • 2 reps per cabin per match, any camper can sub out for a cabinmate in between matches.
  • Default settings
  • 120 second timer
  • Match 1: Turtle Pack, Match 2: Weekly Pack, Match 3: Turtle Pack
  • There shouldn't be any disconnects but if it happens past round 3, we are going to have to play on.


Scoring


Every cabin's TOTAL points will be tallied at the end of the 3 matches, and scoring is based on the team with the lowest average placement.

1st place will be awarded 250 points
2nd place will be awarded 150 points
3rd place will be awarded 100 points
4th place will be awarded 50 points
